Why JAYmath?

This isn't a learning center where your child sits in a room with 20 other kids. Every decision here was made with one thing in mind: your child actually learning.

Max 4 Students Per Hour

Sessions are hard-capped at 4 students. That means your child is seen, heard, and helped throughout the entire session. Not just when they raise their hand.

iPads in the Classroom

Students use educational apps and digital resources on iPads to reinforce what they're learning. It keeps things engaging without being a distraction.

Whiteboard Sessions

Problems get worked out visually, step by step, on a whiteboard. There's no better way to make a concept stick than seeing it broken down in real time.

Snacks Included

Kids come straight from school. A hungry student isn't a focused one. Snacks are provided so that's one less thing between your child and learning.

Supervised the Whole Time

Your child is never left unsupervised. Every session is structured and managed so you can drop them off knowing they're in good hands.

Homework Sent Home

Each student leaves with targeted practice built around where they need work. The session doesn't end when they walk out the door.

What We Cover

Whether your child needs help keeping up or is ready to get ahead, we have a session for that.

Homework Help

Consistent support so your child stays on top of classwork and walks into school ready. No more nightly battles at the kitchen table.

SHSAT Math Prep

Strategic preparation for the Specialized High Schools Admissions Test. We work through the math sections methodically, building the skills and the confidence needed to perform.

NYS Exam Prep

Targeted practice and test-taking strategies for New York State math assessments. Your child will know what to expect and how to approach it.

Summer Head Start

Summer is when a lot of students lose ground. We use that time to sharpen what they know and get them ahead for the year coming up.

Practice Worksheets

Custom worksheets built specifically around each student's weak areas. Not generic packets. Work that's actually relevant to what they need.

Online Work Support

Help navigating digital assignments, school platforms, and tech-based coursework. If it's on a screen and it involves math, we can help.
